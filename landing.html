Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe?

Yes! We're 100% non-custodial. Your keys never leave your wallet. We just build the transactions - you sign everything locally.

What's the fee?

We charge 0.0005 SOL per account closed (~25% of rent). You only pay on successful recovery. Scanning is always free.

Will this affect my tokens?

No! We only close accounts with zero balance. Accounts holding any tokens are never touched.

Which wallets work?

Phantom, Solflare, and Backpack are fully supported. More wallets coming soon.

How long does it take?

Most cleanups complete in under 30 seconds. We batch 6 accounts per transaction for speed.

Is this open source?

The transaction building is transparent and verifiable. You can inspect exactly what you're signing.
